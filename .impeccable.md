## Design Context

### Users
Recruiters, hiring managers, and technical peers reviewing Andy's background in DevOps, Site Reliability Engineering, and Cloud Infrastructure. They need to quickly find key achievements, skills, and contact information.

### Brand Personality
**Vibrant, Fluent, Technical**. The brand communicates confidence, automation, and modern infrastructure expertise.

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Vibrant & Fluent**: A dark-mode first design utilizing deep, rich neutral backgrounds tinted towards the primary brand hue.
- **Accents**: Striking, vibrant OKLCH-based accents for interactive elements.
- **Typography**: `Unbounded` (Display) for a technical, modern, and distinct structural feel. `Manrope` (Body) for crisp readability at small sizes.  
- **Visuals**: Purposeful layering and depth (Fluent design cues) but strictly adhering to impeccable guidelines (no decorative side-stripes, no gradient text, no meaningless glowing cards).
- **Motion**: Purposeful entrance animations, staggered reveals, and optimistic interaction feedback. Smooth ease-out-expo timing.

### Design Principles
1. **Purposeful Depth**: Use fluid shadows and intentional layering to create a sense of architecture (fitting for an Infrastructure/Cloud Engineer).
2. **Distinctive Hierarchy**: Modular type scale with clamp() for headings. Contrast through size and weight rather than just color.
3. **Restraint with Accents**: Use vibrant colors sparingly (10% of the UI) to ensure they have maximum impact when guiding the user to action.
4. **Content as UI**: Let the impressive metrics (e.g., "Reduced deployment times by 40%") speak loudly through asymmetric and confident layouts.
